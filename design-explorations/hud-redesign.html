📊 Design Decisions & Tradeoffs

  • Variant A is the best balance — replaces text-heavy labels with pixel icons, adds the step progress bar inline, and keeps everything in a single compact row. RECOMMENDED
  • Variant B shows ALL 5 pipeline steps simultaneously (like a Kairosoft game status bar). The bottom progress meter adds game-like polish. Best for info-dense monitoring but takes 2x vertical space.
  • Variant C is a floating overlay HUD — semi-transparent, minimal footprint. Useful as an always-visible HUD overlaid on the dungeon floor. Less informative but more atmospheric.
  • Common improvements: Removed redundant labels (pipeline "Cycle:" prefix), consolidated stats into compact groups, added pixel-art progress bars, made buttons smaller and more game-like.
  • Quick win: Even just adding the step progress bar (Variant A's inline bar) to the current HUD is a 5-minute change that significantly improves perceived polish.
